Instructions

Welcome to the Spaceship Asteroid Game! Here's how to play:

  • Use W, A, S, D to move your spaceship.
  • Avoid asteroids falling from the top of the screen.
  • Use projectiles to destroy asteroids.
  • Survive to gain power-ups to enhance your ship:
    • +20% Attack Speed
    • +15% Attack Power
    • +15 HP
    • +20% Projectile Speed
    • +1 Movement Speed
  • Survive as long as possible to maximize your score!
  • Press 1, 2, or 3 to select upgrades when prompted.
