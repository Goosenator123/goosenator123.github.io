Welcome to 2D Animation

  • Dynamic Wave: Generates a moving wave using sine and cosine functions. Adjust the amplitude and frequency via dat.GUI.
  • User Interactions:
    • Right-click to spawn a new ball at the mouse position.
    • Click on a ball to trigger an explosion and remove it.
  • Control Panel (dat.GUI): Real-time control of parameters such as wave amplitude, frequency, gravity, wind, and ball spawning/clearing.
